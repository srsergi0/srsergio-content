Suppose you are a marketing teacher and assign an essay on the importance of market segmentation. A student submits an essay with the following introductory paragraph:

> Market segmentation is a technique in the world of marketing that involves dividing a large market into smaller and more homogeneous groups of consumers. This is done in order to better understand the preferences and needs of each group and offer products and services that fit them. For example, a clothing company can segment its market based on the age and gender of consumers, and offer different lines of clothing for young and older men and women. In this way, it can increase its competitiveness in the market and attract a larger number of consumers.

Would you approve or disapprove? And how would your grade change if you knew that the student did not write the essay at all?

The use of artificial intelligence programs to generate essays is an unacceptable practice that goes against authentic learning and the development of writing skills. Additionally, it promotes cheating and goes against academic norms and values. If a teacher knew that a student did not write an essay at all, the grade would change to a failing grade.

This has been demonstrated recently with the case of a New Zealand student who confessed to using AI to write their assignments. Although the student justified their action by saying that they used AI in the same way as tools like Grammarly or spell check, they were not considered a cheater because the rules of their university only prohibit someone else from doing the work for them. However, as GPT-3 is a program, it cannot be considered a "person" in this case.

## The impact of AI on education

One of the possible radical changes that artificial intelligence technology could bring to education is the use of AI programs to generate assignments. This could be seen as a way to alleviate the workload of teachers and provide personalized learning experiences for students. However, it is important to consider the ethical implications of using AI for this purpose.

The use of AI for generating essays goes against the principles of authentic learning and the development of writing skills. Additionally, it promotes cheating and undermines academic norms and values. It is important for educators and policy makers to address these concerns and establish clear guidelines for the use of AI in education.

> "If Transformer AI systems have a lasting influence on education, perhaps that will come from educators and policymakers having to rethink how they assess students."

## What could be done?

### 1. AI as a learning tool

Firstly, Artificial Intelligence can help students see different ways to express ideas and organize tasks. A teacher could use them in class to create exercises on a topic and then have students analyze and write their own improved versions. This helps them learn from different forms of expression and develop skills to improve their work.

### 2. Fostering creativity

Secondly, AI can also be useful for promoting creativity. For example, students could write a short story using an AI. The student would write the first paragraph, the AI would write the second, and so on. This could help students overcome lack of inspiration and explore different ideas.

### 3. Teaching ethics

Thirdly, teachers can also use these artificial intelligence technologies to talk about ethics and the limits of AI. For example, how does it feel to talk to an expert who only has knowledge in words and has no consciousness of morality or real-world experience? Does a deep neural network have a mind of its own or is it just a machine that processes data?

### 4. Rethinking assessment

Finally, we must ask ourselves if we are really helping students learn by assigning tasks that AI programs can solve. Perhaps it would be more effective to use other forms of assessment, such as constructive feedback or peer evaluation, instead of relying on machines to evaluate students' learning.
